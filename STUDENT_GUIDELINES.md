# Student Guidelines - Learning Management System (LMS)

Welcome to your learning platform! This guide will help you navigate the system and make the most of your learning experience.

---

## 1. Getting Started
- **Accessing the Portal**: Log in using your registered email and password at the login screen.
- **First Impression**: Once logged in, you'll see the **Dashboard**, which provides a 360-degree view of your academic performance.
- **Responsive Design**: The portal works on both Desktop and Mobile. On mobile, use the "Menu" button (three lines) to navigate between sections.
- **Session Stability**: Your login is secured. If you open the portal in a new tab, your session will stay active automatically.

---

## 2. Your Dashboard (Performance Hub)
The Dashboard is your command center. Here you can:
- **Track Progress Circles**: View real-time completion percentages for **Courses**, **Assignments**, and **Tests**. Try to keep these above 80% to stay "Green"!
- **Interactive Leaderboard**:
    - See the Top 3 performers on the podium with special "Rank Badges" and a golden crown for Rank 1.
    - Check your own rank even if you aren't in the top 10 yet—your score is updated instantly after every test.
    - **LinkedIn Integration**: Click the Share icon to open a pre-formatted LinkedIn post. Showcasing your rank to recruiters is a great way to build your professional presence.
- **Live Banners**: Watch the sliding banners for:
    - New course announcements and schedule updates.
    - Success stories and testimonials from placed students.
    - Direct links to important forms (Feedback, Journey Sharing).
- **AI Badges**: Look for the "AI Powered" glowing badge to identify features like automated test evaluation and personalized learning suggestions.

---

## 3. Learning Journey (Courses)
- **Course Library**: Navigate to the **Courses** tab to see all your active and completed batches.
- **Video Lessons**: Click on a course to open the interactive video player. 
    - Videos are organized by topics for easy navigation.
    - The system remembers where you left off in a video.
- **Progress Syncing**: Your "Course Progress" circle on the dashboard updates as you finish watching each module.

---

## 4. Practice & Assessments (Tests)
The **Tests** section is where you validate your knowledge and prepare for technical rounds:
- **Test Categories**: Choose between "Mock Tests" (full simulated exams) or "Topic Tests" (focused on subjects like Java, Python, SQL, etc.).
- **Smart Test Interface**:
    - **Instructions Overlay**: Always read the rules carefully. Common rules include not refreshing the page or exiting full screen.
    - **Countdown Timer**: Keep track of the remaining time at the top of the screen.
    - **Question Navigator**: Use the grid at the side to jump between questions. Colors indicate if a question is "Answered", "Not Answered", or "Not Visited".
- **Advanced Proctoring (Anti-Cheating)**:
    - **Tab Switch Detection**: The system detects if you switch to another tab or minimize the browser.
    - **Warning System**: You will receive a warning modal if a tab switch is detected.
    - **Auto-Submission**: After **3 tab switches**, your test will be automatically submitted with the current progress.
- **Multi-Language Coding Editor**:
    - For coding questions, you can choose between **Java, Python, C++, and JavaScript**.
    - Use the "Run Code" button to see your output against sample test cases before final submission.
- **In-depth Results**: 
    - Get an instant score and percentage right after finishing.
    - **Full Test History**: Access your past scores under the "Test History" sub-tab to see your improvement over time.
    - **Detailed Review**: Re-open completed tests to see which questions you got wrong, with correct answers and explanations.

---

## 5. Assignments & Projects
- **Task Management**: The **Assignments** tab lists projects and coding task sets assigned by your trainers.
- **Performance Overview**: Every assignment includes a dashboard showing:
    - **Total Questions**: Number of tasks in the set.
    - **Solved & Attempted**: Track your completion status.
    - **Marks Obtained**: Total score vs possible marks.
- **Modular Learning**: Assignments are often broken into modules. You can see your progress circle for each module.
- **Company-Linked Tasks**: In the assignment question table, look for **Company Logo Stacks**. These show which top companies (like TCS, Infosys, or Amazon) have asked that specific question in real interviews.
- **Submission & Solving**: 
    - Click **Solve** to enter the coding or MCQ environment.
    - View your "Score Obtained" and "Number of Attempts" for each task individually.

---

## 6. Interview Preparation (Career Tools)
Prepare for your dream job with these dedicated tools:
- **Company Questions**: 
    - Search for questions specific to top tech firms (TCS, Amazon, Google, etc.).
    - Filter questions by difficulty or topic to target your weak areas.
- **Company Documents**: Access exclusive study materials, HR interview guides, resume templates, and placement preparation PDFs.
- **Bookmark System**: Found a crucial question or a helpful document? Click the "Bookmark" icon to save it for a quick revision session later. Access all saved items in the **Bookmarks** tab for quick revision.

---

## 7. Job Opportunities (Placement Cell)
- **Job Drive Tracking**: The **Jobs** tab lists upcoming campus drives and recruitment events.
- **Rich Drive Details**: Click into any job listing to find:
    - **Job Criteria**: Details on salary, work location, and service agreements.
    - **Eligibility Matrix**: Specific requirements for qualification, year of passing, and minimum percentage (10th, 12th, Degree).
    - **Interview Roadmap**: A clear list of interview rounds (e.g., Round 1: Aptitude, Round 2: Technical, Round 3: HR).
    - **Blocking Period**: Information on the cooling-off period if you don't clear the drive.
    - **JD Downloads**: Download the official Job Description PDF directly to your device.
- **Application Status**: Monitor whether you have applied, are shortlisted, or are in the final interview stage.

---

## 8. Managing Your Profile
- **Personalized Stats**: View your overall total score and total tests completed on your profile page.
- **Profile Customization**: Keep your profile image and resume updated to attract more placement opportunities.
- **Secure Exit**: Always use the **Logout** icon in the sidebar when you're done, especially if you're using a shared or public computer.

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Success
1. **Consistency is Key**: Visit the dashboard daily to stay updated on new assignments or job drives.
2. **Review Your Mistakes**: Don't just take tests—always go back to "Test History" to review the questions you got wrong.
3. **Build Your Brand**: Use the LinkedIn sharing feature to let the world know about your coding achievements.
4. **Offline Learning**: Download the **Company Documents** to continue your preparation even without an active internet connection.

Good luck with your learning journey!
